Lasagna Recipe

Description

This lasagna is made with layers of pasta, cheese, and a rich meat sauce.

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Cook Time: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500 gr. ground beef
  • 250ml ricotta cheese
  • 300gr shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 500ml marinara sauce
  • 50gr grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in marinara sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce. Layer with noodles, ricotta cheese, meat sauce, and shredded mozzarella. Repeat layers, ending with meat sauce and a generous sprinkle of Parmesan cheese on top.
  7. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  8. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
